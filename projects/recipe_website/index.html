Pancake

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tablespoons melted butter
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt

Recipe

  1. In a bowl, mix flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.
  2. In another bowl, whisk milk, egg, and melted butter together.
  3. Combine wet and dry ingredients until smooth.
  4. Heat a lightly oiled pan over medium heat and pour batter to form pancakes.
  5. Cook until bubbles form, flip and cook until golden brown.

Hotpot

Ingredients

  • 1 liter chicken or vegetable broth
  • 200g thinly sliced meat (beef or chicken)
  • 100g tofu cubes
  • 1 cup chopped vegetables (bok choy, mushrooms, spinach)
  • 1 packet noodles or vermicelli
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce

Recipe

  1. In a pot, bring the broth to a boil and add garlic and soy sauce.
  2. Add vegetables and let them cook for a few minutes.
  3. Stir in tofu and noodles; cook until noodles are tender.
  4. Add thinly sliced meat and cook until done.
  5. Serve hot directly from the pot with dipping sauces of your choice.

Ramen

Ingredients

  • 4 cups chicken or vegetable broth
  • 2 packs ramen noodles
  • 2 boiled eggs
  • 1 cup sliced mushrooms
  • 1/2 cup chopped green onions
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil

Recipe

  1. Heat broth in a pot and add soy sauce and sesame oil.
  2. Add mushrooms and cook until tender.
  3. Add ramen noodles and cook according to package instructions.
  4. Serve in bowls topped with boiled eggs and green onions.
  5. Enjoy hot!

Pastry

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter (cold and cubed)
  • 1/4 cup cold water
  • 1 tablespoon sugar (for sweet pastry)
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • Filling of your choice (jam, chocolate, cream)
  • 1 egg (for egg wash)

Recipe

  1. In a bowl, mix flour, sugar, and salt.
  2. Cut in the butter until the mixture is crumbly.
  3. Add cold water and mix until dough forms.
  4. Roll out the dough, cut into shapes, and add filling.
  5. Seal edges, brush with egg wash, and bake at 180°C (350°F) for 20–25 minutes.

Takoyaki

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 cups dashi or water
  • 1/2 cup chopped boiled octopus
  • 1/4 cup chopped green onions
  • 1/4 cup pickled ginger (beni shoga)
  • Takoyaki sauce
  • Japanese mayo
  • Bonito flakes (katsuobushi)
  • Seaweed powder (aonori)

Recipe

  1. In a bowl, mix flour, eggs, and dashi to make batter.
  2. Heat a takoyaki pan and grease it well.
  3. Pour batter into the molds and add octopus, ginger, and green onions.
  4. Turn the balls with skewers until golden and crisp all around.
  5. Top with takoyaki sauce, mayo, bonito flakes, and seaweed powder.
